The mod adds additional slots for Weapons, Upgrades and Skills/Consumables.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mech can equip 6 weapons in each hand with the mod.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod adds 12 new slots for upgrades (a total of 16).

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod adds 2 new skills and consumables slots (total becomes 10).

Two new slots skills and consumables are bonded to keys 9 and 0.
