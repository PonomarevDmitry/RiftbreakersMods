The mod adds new skill "Wall Breaker".

Wall Breaker skill generates a seismic wave that destroys destructible walls in the caverns biome around Mr Riggs.

It is necessary to hold the skill activation button for 3 seconds to release a seismic wave.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

New skill will appear in the new game and in saves.

Localizations:
- English
- Russian
- Chinese (Great thanks STAR-KY-XH @ discord)
