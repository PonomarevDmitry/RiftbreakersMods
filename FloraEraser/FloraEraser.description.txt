The mod adds Flora Eraser Tool to remove plants.

Flora Eraser Tool allows the player to remove plants that cannot be destroyed by power jump or explosives.

Flora Eraser Tool allows the player to remove burned plants.

Plants will not grow back after the plants are removed with Flora Eraser Tool.

Flora Eraser Tool ignores Poogret Food Plants.

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

New tool will appear in the new game and in saves.
