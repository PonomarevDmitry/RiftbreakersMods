The mod adds two new tools "Loot Collecting Tool" and "All Loot Collecting Tool".

"Loot Collecting Tool" allows player to quickly collect loot from the selected area without running around it or using a loot drone.

"All Loot Collecting Tool" allows player to quickly collect loot from the entire map.

The tools can be useful in collecting loot after a big battle with creatures.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

New tools will appear in the new game and in saves.

- Chinese (Great thanks STAR-KY-XH @ discord)
