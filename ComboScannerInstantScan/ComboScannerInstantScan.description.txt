The mod makes Combo Scanner's scanning instant.

The mod is compatible with all "Combo Scanner" sub-mods.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Combo Scanner" mod is required.

"Combo Scanner" mod:

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3573599790

https://mod.io/g/riftbreaker/m/combo-scanner

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

Updated to Riftbreaker 2.0.
Tested in Multiplayer mode.
