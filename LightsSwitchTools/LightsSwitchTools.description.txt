The mod adds tools to turn on and off the lighting of buildings and Mr. Riggs.


"Turn Light On" tool allows you to turn on the lighting of selected buildings.
"Turn Light Off" tool allows you to turn off the lighting of selected buildings.


"Light Switching in All Buildings Tool" allows player to turn lights on or off in all buildings.
Use mouse wheel to change the operating mode of the tool.


"Mech's Lamps Switcher Tool" allows player to turn Mr. Riggs's lamps on and off.


The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

New tools will appear in the new game and in saves.
