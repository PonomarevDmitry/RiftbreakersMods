The mod adds 10 new automatic versions of standard weapons.

New versions of standard weapons:
- Blaster Auto
- Bouncing Blades Auto
- Burst Rifle Auto
- Charged Bomb Auto
- Heavy Plasma Auto
- High Caliber Rifle Auto
- Immolator Auto
- Mortar Auto
- Nuclear Missile Auto
- Railgun Auto
- Repeater Rifle Auto
- Rocket Launcher Auto
- Shotgun Auto

The new weapon does not have a charging function. The new weapon will fire all the time while holding the firing button.

The mod adds a new research tree that unlocks new automatic weapons.
New research will be automatically completed when standard weapon research is completed.

The characteristics of the new weapon and the cost of crafting are similar to standard ones.

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

New weapons will appear in the new game and in saves.

Changed Localizations:
- English
- Russian
- Chinese (Great thanks STAR-KY-XH @ discord)

All other localizations are standard.

Updated to Riftbreaker to 2.0.
