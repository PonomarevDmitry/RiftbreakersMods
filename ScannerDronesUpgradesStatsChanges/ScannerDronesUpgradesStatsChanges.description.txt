The mod changes the bonus characteristics of the Bioscanner and Geoscanner Equipment.

Bioscanner Equipment now gives additional health and health regeneration.

Geoscanner Equipment now gives additional forcefield and forcefield regeneration.

"Scanner Drones" mod is required.

The mod is compatible with all "Scanner Drones" sub-mods.

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.
