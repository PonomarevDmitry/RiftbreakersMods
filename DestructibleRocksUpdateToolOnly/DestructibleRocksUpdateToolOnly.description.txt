This mod is an update of the Destructible Rocks, which is outdated.

The mod makes Rocks in Cryo Fields Destructible.

The mod makes Giant Arachneum Mushrooms in Swamp Destructible.



Original Mod:
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2889031236

Indestructible rocks are now destructible!
Player needs to use powerful enough weapons like a hammer or rocket launcher to destroy them.
Works only for new maps generated with this Mod turned on (rocks in old save files will not be destructible).
This can cause weird enemy behaviour and situation with objects floating in the air.

Updated to WE4.
