This Experimental mod displays information about the creature's resistances and vulnerabilities above the creatures.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Information is displayed only if the player attacks a creature.
If the tower is attacking a creature, then the information is not displayed.

If several creatures of the same type are attacked, the information will not be displayed for all creatures, but only for single one.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Information is displayed if the player attacks a creature with a damage type with an effectiveness less or equal than 100%.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The information will be displayed for 5 seconds and will be extended when the creature takes damage again.
The information will be displayed for another 3 seconds after the creature's death.
