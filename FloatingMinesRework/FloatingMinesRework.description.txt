The mod improves Floating Mines weapons.

Standard Floating Mines Weapons place 1 mine.
Advanced Floating Mines Weapons place 2 mines.
Superior Floating Mines Weapons place 3 mines.
Extreme Floating Mines Weapons place 4 mines.
