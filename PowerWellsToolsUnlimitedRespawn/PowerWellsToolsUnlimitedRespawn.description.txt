The mod is a submod of mod "Power Wells Saving Tools" and activates the feature of Unlimited respawn of Power Wells after saving one.

The player gets the opportunity to create an unlimited number of Power Wells after saving one.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Power Wells Saving Tools" mod is required.

The mod is compatible with all "Power Wells Saving Tools" sub-mods.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

Updated to Riftbreaker 2.0.
