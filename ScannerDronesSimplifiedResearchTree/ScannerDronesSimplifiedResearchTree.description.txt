The mod simplifies the Scanner Drones research tree.

It is necessary to research only "Alien Research" before researching the Upgrade "Bioscanner Equipment".

It is necessary to research only "Cobalt Handling" before researching the Upgrade "Geoscanner Equipment".

"Scanner Drones" mod is required.

The mod is compatible with all "Scanner Drones" sub-mods.

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.
