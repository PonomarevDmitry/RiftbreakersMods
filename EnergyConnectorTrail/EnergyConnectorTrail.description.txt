The mod adds new skill Energy Connector Trail.

Energy connectors are built along the path of the mech-suit while the skill is working.

Extra Stable Floor will be built on quicksand before building the energy connector.

The skill icon is displayed above the mech-suit while the skill is working.

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

New skill will appear in the new game and in saves.

Localizations:
- English
- Russian
- Chinese (Great thanks STAR-KY-XH @ discord)

Updated to DLC3.
