The mod adds new skill "Mech's Lamps Switcher".

"Mech's Lamps Switcher" turns Mr. Riggs's lamps on and off.


The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

New skill will appear in the new game and in saves.

Localizations:
- English
- Russian
- Chinese (Great thanks STAR-KY-XH)
