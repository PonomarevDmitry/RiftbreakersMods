The mod changes Mine Layer Towers and allows the player to change the action area of mine layer drones.
The mod changes Mine Layer Towers and allows the player to select the types of mines to place.

To change the drone action area, select the building (the action building menu appears), move the cursor to the point and move the mouse wheel down.



----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod adds mod slots to Mine Layer Towers:
- Level 1 has 1 slots
- Level 2 has 2 slots
- Level 3 has 3 slots

The mod slots can be filled with a mine type mod:
- Small Mine
- Acid Mine
- Cryogenic Mine
- Energy Mine
- Gravity Mine
- Holo Mine
- Incendiary Mine
- Nuclear Mine
- Sonic Mine
The default mine type is Small Mine. If the mod slot is not filled, Small Mines will be placed.

The mod adds new research tree that unlocks new mine types.
New research will be automatically completed when required research is completed.

All types of mines require research "Heavy Artillery Towers – Level 1".
Mine types require the following research or global awards:
- Small Mine does not require other researches
- Acid Mine require "Corrosive Gun – Standard" research
- Cryogenic Mine require "Cryogenic Mine – Standard" research
- Energy Mine require "Energy Weapons – Standard" research
- Gravity Mine require "Gravity Mine" item any quality
- Holo Mine require "Holo Decoy" item any quality
- Incendiary Mine require "Liquid Weapons – Advanced" research
- Nuclear Mine require "Nuclear Mine – Standard" research
- Sonic Mine require "Sonic Grenades - Standard" research

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod adds tools for coping mods from one tower to another.

Mine Layer Tower Mods Picker Tool saves mods from the selected tower for insertion into another.
If saved mods and current mods in a tower match, the Mine Layer Tower Mods Picker Tool does not highlight the tower.
Use mouse wheel to select the previous selected mods.

Mine Layer Tower Mods Replacer Tool replaces the mods with the saved ones in all selected towers.
If saved mods and current mods in a tower match, the Mine Layer Tower Mods Replacer Tool does not highlight the tower.

Icons and names of the saved mods are displayed above the selection area.

Tools only work with Mine Layer Towers.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod adds Move Mine Layer Tower Drone Area Tool that moves drone action area to another point.
The tool allows the player to select many buildings and move their drone action areas.

1. To add or remove a building to the list, click on a building or on an drone action area point.

Buildings in the list are highlighted in blue.
When player hover over a building, the building will be highlighted in green or red.
- The building that will be added to the list is highlighted in green.
- The building that will be removed from the list is highlighted in red.

The drone action area points of the selected buildings will be highlighted in green.

2. To clear the list of buildings, move the mouse wheel up.

3. To move the drone action area points of the selected buildings, move the mouse wheel down.
