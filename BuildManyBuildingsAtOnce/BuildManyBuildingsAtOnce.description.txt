Build many buildings at once



The mod allows the player to build many buildings by holding the left mouse button.

Now the player can build many buildings without fear that one of them will move and ruin the line.

Now if a building blueprint is colored yellow, it means that this building will overwrite another building.
In this case, both the new building and the removed building will be colored yellow.

A building colored yellow can be built.
The yellow color is just a signal for convenience.

Changed files:
- lua/misc/ghost.lua
- lua/misc/ghost_building.lua




一键批量建造 Original

mod允许玩家通过按住鼠标左键来建造许多建筑。
现在玩家可以建造许多建筑，而不用担心其中一座会移动并破坏线路。
现在，如果一个建筑蓝图是黄色的，这意味着这个建筑将覆盖另一个建筑。
在这种情况下，新建筑和拆除的建筑都将被涂成黄色。
可以建造一座黄色的建筑。
黄色只是为了方便起见的一个信号。
