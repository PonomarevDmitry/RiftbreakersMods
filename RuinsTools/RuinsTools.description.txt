The mod adds two tools that allow the player to sell buildins and place ruins and to erase ruins.

Sell and Place Ruins Tool sells buildings and leaves ruins in place of buildings.
The tool allows the player to quickly sell an outpost before attacking creatures and quickly build an outpost in its original form.

Ruins Eraser Tool removes ruins.

When using Sell and Place Ruins Tool and Ruins Eraser Tool, ruins are highlighted in gray.

The mod also changes Repair Tool and Sell Tool.

When using Repair Tool and Sell Tool, ruins are highlighted in gray.

New tools will appear in the new game and in saves.

Changed files:
- lua/misc/repair_tool.lua
- lua/misc/sell_tool.lua
