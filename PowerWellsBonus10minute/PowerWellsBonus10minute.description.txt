The mod contains a script that increases the duration of the Team Boost Bonus by six times.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Increased Team Boost duration for 2 players: from 30 seconds to 180 seconds.

Increased Team Boost duration for 3 players: from 40 seconds to 240 seconds.

Increased Team Boost duration for 4 or more players: from 50 seconds to 300 seconds.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

Updated to Riftbreaker 2.0.
Tested in Multiplayer mode.
