The mod is a submod of mod "Power Wells Saving Tools" and activates the feature of respawning 5 Power Wells after saving one.

The player gets the opportunity to create 5 Power Wells after saving one "wild" Power Well.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Note: The player gets the opportunity to create only 1 Power Well after saving one created by "Power Wells Respawn" Tool.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Power Wells Saving Tools" mod is required.

The mod is compatible with all "Power Wells Saving Tools" sub-mods.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

Updated to Riftbreaker 2.0.
