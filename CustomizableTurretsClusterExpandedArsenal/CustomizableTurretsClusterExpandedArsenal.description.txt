The mod adds new research tree that unlocks EA turrets types for use in skills "Turrets Cluster" 1-3.

New research will be automatically completed when required research is completed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Customizable Turrets Cluster" mod is required.
"Expanded Arsenal" mod is required.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.
