Mod increases the area of the tools to 32.

The area of the original tools can be 1, 2, 4, 8.

Now the area of the tools can be of the following sizes:
- 1
- 2
- 4
- 8
- 12
- 16
- 20
- 24
- 28
- 32


Affected original vanilla tools:
- Floor Eraser
- Repair Tool
- Sell Tool
- Power Switch Tool
- Upgrade Tool

The mod will change the size of the area of other mods tools.



Changed files:
- lua/misc/tool.lua
