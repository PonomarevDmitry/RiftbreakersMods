The mod makes energy lines invisible.

If the player uses mod "Unlimited Energy Connector Range", then a lot of ugly lines appear around connectors.
This mod allows the player to make the lines invisible.


Mod "Unlimited Energy Connector Range" Author Staalby
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2890098792
https://mod.io/g/riftbreaker/m/unlimited-energy-connector-range

The mod will not conflict with any other mods as all files in this mod are new.

Updated to DLC2.
