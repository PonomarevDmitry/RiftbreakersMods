Mod reduces the width of the tabs in the research menu by half.

The mod can help you if you have a lot of mods with different research trees.

If there are more than 13 research trees, scroll buttons appear.

The mod reduces the width of tabs only in the research menu. The tabs in the statistics menu and the mods menu remain unchanged.

Changed files:
- gui/menu/research.gui







Mod将研究菜单中选项卡的宽度减少了一半。

如果您有很多具有不同研究树的模组，该模组可以为您提供帮助。

如果研究树超过 13 个，则会出现滚动按钮。

该模组仅在研究菜单中减小选项卡的宽度。统计菜单和模组菜单中的选项卡保持不变。

更改的文件：
- gui/menu/research.gui
